Other Ways to Give

Donate In-Kind:

Your thoughtful contributions of goods and supplies help bring comfort, learning, and joy to children fighting cancer. You can donate:


  • New toys, board games, or art supplies for our playroom
  • Children’s books and educational materials for our classroom
  • Clothes, blankets, and hygiene items for patients and families
  • Snacks or gift packs for ward celebrations and events

To arrange a drop-off or pickup, please reach out to our team — we’ll be happy to coordinate with you.

Donation FAQs

Quick answers about giving and impact.

How can I donate?

You can donate in-kind, volunteer your time, or contact us to learn about upcoming giving opportunities.

Can I visit the foundation?

Yes! We welcome visitors by appointment. Reach out to us to plan your visit and meet the team.

Where does my donation go?

Your contributions directly support our hospital programs, events, and emotional care for children and families.

Can I donate supplies instead of money?

Absolutely! Items like toys, books, clothes, or art materials are always welcome for our classrooms and events.
