FAQs

Answers to some of the most common questions about CCCF and our work in Pakistan.

What does CCCF do?

CCCF provides emotional, educational, and logistical support to children fighting cancer and their families in Pakistan. We work to make their journey less frightening and more hopeful through a range of psychosocial programs.

How can I contribute?

You can donate funds, volunteer, or contribute in-kind — such as toys, books, and supplies. Every form of help allows us to reach more families and provide more care where it’s needed most.

Where do my donations go?

Your contributions directly support children’s therapy, family counseling, hospital-school operations, and community events that restore joy and belonging. We’re transparent about how every rupee is used to bring hope.

Can I visit or volunteer?

Absolutely! You can contact us to learn about volunteering opportunities in hospitals or events. Whether you bring your skills, time, or simply your compassion — your presence matters.
