FAQs

Discover answers to common questions about our foundation and how we support childhood cancer care.

What is your mission?

Our mission is to provide comprehensive support for children battling cancer and their families. We aim to enhance their quality of life through various programs and services. Together, we strive to raise awareness and funds for childhood cancer research.

How can I help?

You can help by making a donation, volunteering your time, or participating in our events. Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference in the lives of children with cancer. Join us in our mission to support these brave young fighters.

What events do you host?

We host a variety of events throughout the year, including charity runs, galas, and awareness campaigns. These events not only raise funds but also foster community engagement and support. Check our events page for upcoming opportunities to get involved.

Where do donations go?

Donations directly support our programs that provide medical assistance, emotional support, and educational resources for families. We ensure that every dollar is used effectively to make a positive impact. Transparency is key, and we provide regular updates on how funds are allocated.

How can I volunteer?

Volunteering with us is a rewarding experience that allows you to make a tangible difference. You can sign up on our website to learn about available opportunities. Whether you help at events or provide support in other ways, your involvement is invaluable.
